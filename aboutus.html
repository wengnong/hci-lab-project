HISTORY

Founded in 1998, DiamondCut Motors gives a spectacular performance in automotive field as a prestigious car reseller. DiamondCut Motors provides luxurious vehicles throughout Indonesia. DiamondCut Motors offers various renowned brands such as Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, Bugatti, and many others. A number of business owners and successful individuals have been customers of DiamondCut Motors, drawn by its cutting-edge service, timely delivery, and vehicle safety.

MISSION

DiamondCut Motors desires to inspire customers through our spectacular automotive technologies and exceptional service. We are dedicated to reach more customers globally, offering products that enhance their driving experience. Hence, our aim is to make our innovative solutions accessible to wider audience who has the same interest in the automotive spectrum.

VISION

At DiamondCut Motors, our vision is to become a global leader in the automotive industry by expanding our reach and impact worldwide. Our goal is to drive massive expansion with strong presence in automotive markets by expanding our impact worldwide. By doing so, we hope to set new standards where excellence and sustainability can be reached through interested customers or even in the automotive industry.
